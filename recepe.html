Brocolli Sauté

45 mins

easy

cheap

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • 120
  • g
  • Olive Oil
  • 8
  • ml
  • Onion
  • 1
  • pc
  • Broccoli
  • 50
  • g
  • Blue Cheese
  • 20
  • g
  • Garlic
  • 1
  • pcs
  • Milk
  • 10
  • ml
  • Salt, BP, others
  • 0.01
  • mg

Instructions:

  1. Step: In a larger pot, fry finely chopped onions in hot oil until vitreous.
  2. Step: Add chopped chicken breast into small pieces.
  3. Step: Season the meat and let it simmer over a weaker flame for about 20 minutes.
  4. Step: In the second saucepan, pour the chopped and washed brocolli into smaller pieces with 1 cup of hot water.
  5. Step: Simmer for about 10 minutes - until softened.
  6. Step: Then mix the brocolli with the water left in the pot.
  7. Step: Incorporate blue cheese, crushed garlic cloves and salt into the resulting puree.
  8. Step: If necessary, add a little milk. When the meat is ready, mix it with the resulting brocolli sauce.
